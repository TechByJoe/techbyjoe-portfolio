Reverse Engineered Remote Controlled Car to Dough Mixer Project

This model was part of a reverse engineering project where the task was to utilize as many pieces of an RC car as possible to design something entirely new. I chose to use these constraints to create a dough mixer with a built-in battery as a backup source of power. I used the power button from the car's remote transmitter, the joystick as a speed controller, and two LEDs for power indicators. Not pictured are internals such as the battery, circuit board, two rotors, and a connective part.
