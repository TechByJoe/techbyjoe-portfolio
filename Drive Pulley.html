Washing Machine Drive Pulley Replacement

As another personal project, I designed a temporary drive pulley replacement part for a washing machine. In creating this model, I aimed to use as little material as possible, as the pulley only needed to be able to drive a belt. I made a circular pattern of arched support beams to ensure the part was durable but also lightweight.
