Knife Handle

This design was a personal project where I re-created a handle out of wood PLA for a kitchen knife. I started by importing a photo of the bare metal knife into Fusion 360, then designed the handle to match the curvature of the base. After printing multiple test prints in regular PLA, I printed the finalized model in composite wood PLA. I then sanded and polished each side of the handle with wood finish to complete the realistic wooden look. The two sides were held together using an epoxy to ensure they bond firmly to the metal of the knife's base.
